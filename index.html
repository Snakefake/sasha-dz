CONCERT MEDIA and the Hungarian National Symphony Orchestra Szeged proudly present the first edition of the International Ferenc Fricsay Conducting Competition (IFFCC)that will be held in the beautiful city of Szeged (Hungary) and will consist of 2 phases:
01
The ELIMINATION PHASE
evaluation of the candidates' video and CVs by the jury committee. DEADLINE: JUNE 1, 2023. 100 participants will be chosen and announced on JULY
1, 2023
02
The ELIMINATION PHASE
evaluation of the candidates' video and CVs by the jury committee. DEADLINE: JUNE 1, 2023. 100 participants will be chosen and announced on JULY 1, 2023
Prize Winners will receive guest conducting engagements with outstanding orchestras such as the Royal Philharmonic Orchestra London, Royal Liverpool Philharmonic Orchestra, Leipzig Symphony Orchestra, Hungarian National Symphony Orchestra (Szeged), Bulgarian National Radio Symphony Orchestra, Philharmonisches Staatsorchester Mainz and Craiova “Oltenia” Philharmonic Orchestra. The IFFCC offers
great career opportunities and guarantees an impressive media focus to each participant.
